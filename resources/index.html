Meditations

The following are guided meditations, narrated by me. May they be of benefit to you.

Brief Self-Compassion: this short meditation is intended to instill a sense of self-compassion by relating to yourself as you would to a good friend. This meditation is adapted from the work of Kristen Neff, PhD.

Soften, Soothe, Allow: this is a meditation for dealing with difficult emotions.This meditation is adapted from the work of Kristen Neff, PhD.

Compassionate Body Scan: this is a relaxation meditation. It is intended to bring focus and acceptance to the body.

Tonglen: this is an ancient Buddhist meditation. It is intended for working with painful sensations or emotions, and is designed to increase your sense of belonging and connection.
