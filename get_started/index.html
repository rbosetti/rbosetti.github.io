Get Started

You do not need to know your diagnosis or treatment goals to get in touch, as that is often a big part of our initial work together. All you have to know is what in your life is concerning or confusing to you. I would be glad to provide a brief initial phone consultation to address any questions you might have.

Get in Touch

When you are ready to get started, you can either call me directly at (347) 735-9740 or email me at hello@compasscbt.com (fill out this contact form) to schedule your first appointment.

I will do my best to respond to your inquiry as soon as possible. Please note that it may take me longer to contact you by phone versus email. An immediate response cannot be guaranteed by either phone or email.

If you are experiencing an emergency please call 911 or proceed to your nearest emergency room.

Session Format

The first 50 minute appointment will focus on getting to know you, gathering information about your current problems and concerns, and discussing goals for therapy. By the end of the evaluation, we will develop a specialized treatment plan that addresses your goals for therapy. Traditional weekly 45-50 minute appointments are available and recommended. This frequency allows us to build momentum. You can also request more frequent sessions or double sessions. We will discuss the frequency and timing of sessions that best suits your needs.

Virtual Sessions

I offer safe and secure, HIPAA-compliant, virtual therapy to California and New York residents. You can use this option for occasional sessions or for every session. We can discuss the pros and cons of virtual therapy for your particular situation during our initial consultation.

Payment and Cancellation Policy

I accept cash, check, and credit cards for payment. Due to the very limited nature of appointments, you will be charged the full fee for sessions cancelled with less than 48 hours notice.

Insurance

I am an “out-of-network” provider which means that I do not accept insurance directly. However, many insurance companies will reimburse you for some portion of the fees with the proper documentation. You can call your insurance provider in advance to see how much they will reimburse. I try to make the process as easy as possible by providing all of the necessary paperwork upon request, including payment receipts and diagnosis codes.
