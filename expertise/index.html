Areas of Expertise

While some people seek treatment for specific mental health conditions, it is not necessary to know or even meet criteria for a mental health diagnosis in order to benefit from therapy. I recognize that therapy is a very personal process and you are a complex human being. While I have the expertise to assess symptoms and provide appropriate diagnoses, I also go above and beyond to treat each client as an individual. In our early sessions I will conduct a thorough assessment in order to understand your social and cultural background, life experiences, circumstances, struggles and concerns. Together, we will set goals guided by your values and priorities, and I will devise a treatment plan to help you reach them. We will discuss the plan in detail; you will always know what we are doing and why. I will guide and support you every step of the way.
